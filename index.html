I bring a depth to my practice that only years of experience and a life time of curiosity can create. I truly enjoy the creative process of therapy and I bring that to my clients and their families. Often the journey of change in therapy starts from a painful place, yet quickly, with skill, one can find joy in that change. I am committed to building healthy relationships, one that you and your family are at the center. Therapy is about establishing a trusting relationship, exploring aspects of oneself that need to change and grow. I am a respectful and active partner in that process. I draw from a rich and diverse theoretical background, using creativeness, a directness and a gentleness in our discussions. It is an opportunity to create a whole human being. Come work with me.
