Kim is on most insurance plans as a provider, or is enrolled in most Employee Assistance plans in this area. Often you are able to use your insurance for psychotherapy, to assist in offsetting cost. Initial authorizations are often required and you start this with a call to your carrier. This information can be found on your insurance card or through your human resources department. He will accept your co-pay at each session and will bill your carrier though our billing staff. They make every attempt to collect the balance due and do a great job keeping up with all the insurance requirements. But keep in mind all charges are ultimately your responsibility.

Rates & Payments

Cash, Checks Credit cards are accepted.

Cancellation Policy

If you must cancel your session, please call 24 hours in advance. Try to reschedule with the staff during the business day, it is the most efficient way to stay on track. If you do not come to your session or have not cancelled, you will incur a charge of $60.00.

Service Plans

Services may be covered in part by your insurance plan, companies benefit plan, Employee Assistance Plan*.

Please call your insurance plan to ask these questions:

  • Do I have mental health benefits?
  • What is my deductible and has it been met?
  • How many sessions per calendar year does my plan cover?
  • What is the coverage amount per therapy session?
  • Is Kim Taylor “in network” or on my panel?
  • How much does my plan cover for an out-of-network provider?
*Services not covered by your insurance will be billed directly to you. Please ask about a purchase of a book of sessions, if you plan on choosing a Fee for Service plan.
